## Production rules

- Produce only these 30 layouts.
- A completed layout must pass self-review before its checklist item is marked complete.
- Do not mark drafts, rejected versions, or temporary files as complete.
- Save approved PNG files in `storyboard/master-layouts/`.
- Keep version history when replacing an approved layout.
- After all 30 are complete, review wide / medium / close-up balance, lens balance, composition repetition, and Seedance suitability.
